Cost Breakdown & Assumptions

Salary costs are structured to support the company's growth, with foundational salaries for founders and market-rate compensation for new hires starting in Q2 (Nov 2025).

  • Founders (2): ₹15 K/month each.
  • App Developers (2): ₹80 K/month each.
  • Backend Developer: ₹80 K/month.
  • Machine Learning Developer: ₹1.5 Lacs/month.
  • UI/UX Designer: ₹40 K/month.
  • ML Interns (3): ₹10 K/month each.

The marketing strategy involves a grassroots campus program and scalable digital advertising, with a dedicated manager to oversee efforts from Q2.

  • Campus CEOs: Starts with 10 CEOs in Q1 (₹1 K stipend + ₹150 incentive each), scaling to 100 CEOs from Q2 onwards.
  • Ad Spend: Begins at ₹25 K/month in Q1, increasing to ₹40 K/month from Q2.
  • Prizes: ₹50 K/month for ongoing contests, with an additional one-time prize of ₹1 Lac in the third month of Q1 (October).

Infrastructure costs are planned to scale with user growth and increasing technical demands, particularly for backend and AI/ML services.

  • Tech Tools: Fixed at ₹10 K/month for essential software (Jira, Figma, Slack, etc.).
  • Office/Ops: Fixed at ₹15 K/month for co-working space and supplies.
  • Backend/Server: Scales quarterly from ₹8 K/month in Q1 to ₹25 K/month in Q4 to support growth on platforms like AWS or GCP.
  • Legal & Compliance: A one-time cost of ₹50 K in Q1 for initial setup.
